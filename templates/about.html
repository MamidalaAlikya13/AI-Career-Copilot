🎯 Project Vision

Many students struggle with placement preparation because they are unsure about required skills, resume quality, suitable projects, and interview readiness. AI Career Copilot brings all these needs into one intelligent platform.

The goal of this project is to provide personalized career guidance through specialized AI-driven modules such as Resume Expert, Interview Coach, Skill Gap Analyst, Roadmap Planner, and Project Mentor.

✨ Core Features

📄 AI Resume Expert

Upload resumes and receive ATS-style score, strengths, weak areas, missing skills, role recommendations, and resume improvement suggestions.

🤖 AI Career Mentor

Ask role-specific career questions and get expert-style guidance for AI, development, data, cloud, security, and other roles.

🚀 Roadmap Generator

Generate 30-day, 3-month, 6-month, or 12-month learning roadmaps based on your selected career role and current skills.

🎤 Mock Interview System

Practice role-based interview questions with difficulty levels, instant scoring, and feedback for improvement.

🎯 Skill Gap Analyzer

Compare current skills with target role requirements and identify missing skills with improvement recommendations.

💡 AI Project Recommender

Get beginner, intermediate, advanced, or mixed project ideas for different roles with AI-generated unique suggestions.

📊 Placement Analytics

Track resume score, interview attempts, readiness level, and overall placement preparation progress.

🌐 Portfolio Generator

Generate professional portfolio content to showcase skills, projects, certifications, and achievements.

🗂 History Tracking

Store and review interview history, resume analysis records, and user progress data for continuous improvement.

🧭 How It Works

Step 1: Create an Account

Register and access your personalized AI Career Copilot dashboard.

Step 2: Analyze Your Resume

Upload your resume and get ATS-style score, strengths, gaps, and improvements.

Step 3: Identify Skill Gaps

Select your target role and compare your current skills with required skills.

Step 4: Generate Roadmap

Create a clear learning plan for 30 days, 3 months, 6 months, or 12 months.

Step 5: Practice Interviews

Prepare with role-specific mock interview questions and performance feedback.

Step 6: Build Projects

Use the project recommender to build resume-worthy projects and deploy them online.
