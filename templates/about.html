🚀 AI Career Copilot

AI Career Copilot is an intelligent placement preparation and career guidance platform developed to help students assess their skills, improve their resumes, prepare for interviews, generate personalized career roadmaps, and build industry-ready portfolios.

🎯 Project Vision

Many students struggle with placement preparation because they do not know what skills they are missing, which projects to build, or how to prepare effectively. AI Career Copilot solves this problem by bringing resume analysis, interview practice, skill assessment, project recommendations, and portfolio generation into a single platform.

✨ Core Features

📄 Resume Analyzer

Upload resumes and receive ATS scores, skill analysis, keyword matching, and improvement suggestions.

🤖 AI Mentor Chat

Ask career-related questions and receive AI-powered guidance, learning recommendations, and placement advice.

🚀 Career Roadmap Generator

Generate structured learning paths based on your dream role and career goals.

🎤 Mock Interview System

Practice interview questions and receive performance feedback to improve communication and technical skills.

📊 Placement Analytics

Track ATS scores, interview performance, readiness level, and overall placement preparation progress.

🎯 Skill Gap Analyzer

Compare current skills against target roles and identify missing skills that need improvement.

💡 Project Recommendation Engine

Get role-specific project ideas, required skills, learning resources, and salary insights.

🌐 Portfolio Generator

Create professional portfolio content to showcase skills, projects, and achievements.
